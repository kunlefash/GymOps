# Step 02: Discover Target Users

## Instructions
1. Based on the problem statement, identify user personas
2. For each persona, understand goals, frustrations, and context

<ask>
Now let's understand your users deeply:

1. Who are the primary users? (e.g., gym owners, trainers, members, front desk staff)
2. For each user type:
   - What's their daily workflow related to this problem?
   - What frustrates them most?
   - What tools do they currently use?
3. Who is the decision-maker vs. the end user?
4. Are there secondary users we should consider?
</ask>
